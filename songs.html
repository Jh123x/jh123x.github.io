    Pros

    1. Catchy Tune in the songs
    2. Nice voices in the singers
    3. Music video is relavant to the song
    4. Lyrics are prominent in the video
    Cons

    1. The lyrics do not have any deep meaning within the song
    2. Cliched music video involving couples
    Meaning behind the song

    The song is about a couple who met in the past and got into a relationship. However, their relationship grew apart once the girl have to leave for another country. However after 4 years they are reunited in a hotel bar and got back together.
